01 — Deliverables

Why this course exists

Because AI readiness should not be limited to Computer Science students only.

Problem 01

AI feels abstract for non-CS learners

Students from Arts, Commerce, and general graduation streams hear AI terms but cannot map them to their own future path.

Fix: practical demos in Bangla-English with relatable use-cases

Problem 02

No definite pathway

Most learners jump between random videos and tools, then quit because they don't know what to use first and what they should build.

Fix: a structured 2-week pathway with day-wise tasks

02 — Deliverables

Two weekends. Two real builds.

Not slides about AI — things you can show your parents, teachers, and future employers.
